Veggie Bowl

Description

Dinner for 2

This is a simple meal but one we use at our house fairly often. I like it because it's basically just bare necessities and it's easy to make so long as you have an oven/microwave. It usually feeds two as we split the potato between the both of us.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potato
  • Microwaveable Pouch of Mixed Rice & Quinoa
  • Plant-based Chicken Nuggets of Your Choice
  • 3 Cups of Spinach
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • (optional) Seasonings of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Prick holes in your potato to let steam escape as it cooks (alternatively, cut potato into slices)
  3. When oven is ready, cook potato for 50-60 minutes
  4. Put spinach into a bowl, squeeze lemon onto spinach, then toss
  5. Towards the end of the potato's time in the oven, cook desired amount of chicken nuggets as directed by the box they came in
  6. Slice cooked nuggets in half
  7. Microwave rice & quinoa mixture as directed on package then add to bowl
  8. Slice avocado
  9. Once potato is done cooking, cut in half and add each half to a plate/bowl for serving
  10. Divvy up the spinach/rice/quinoa, chicken nuggets, and avocado amongst serving plates/bowls
  11. Add your desired seasonings and enjoy!
