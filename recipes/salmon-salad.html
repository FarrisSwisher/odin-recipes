Salmon with Pesto Kale Salad

Description

Dinner for 2

An easy dinner where most of the work is done simply making the salad.

Ingredients

  • 10-15 oz of Salmon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lemon Pepper
  • 6 cups of Kale
  • 1 tbsp of Pesto
  • 1 Green Onion
  • 1/2 cup candied pecans
  • (optional) Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. A little over an hour before you plan to eat, start making the salad
  2. Take your kale (already rinsed) and throw into a salad bowl
  3. Chop the green onion and add into the bowl with your kale
  4. Take your candied pecans and mash them up (how you achieve this is up to you, really); add to bowl
  5. (optional) Add red pepper flakes to the salad for a little kick
  6. Add your pesto to the bowl, toss salad thoroughly so that you massage the kale in the process (prevents the kale from being too tough to digest)
  7. Place salad in the refrigerator for 1 hour (roughly)
  8. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F
  9. Take your salmon and rinse it with water
  10. Place salmon on desired cooking sheet/tray
  11. Season salmon with a few cranks of salt, pepper, and a healthy sprinkle of lemmon pepper
  12. Cook salmon for 20-25 minutes so that you can see the fat come out of the sides
  13. Serve
